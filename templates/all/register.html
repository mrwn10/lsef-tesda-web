Terms and Conditions
Enrollment and Training
  1. All applicants must meet the specific requirements for their chosen program.
  2. Training schedules are fixed and must be strictly followed.
  3. Regular attendance is mandatory (minimum 80% attendance required).
  4. Students must complete all required competencies to receive certification.
Student Conduct
  1. Maintain proper decorum and respect towards instructors and fellow students.
  2. Proper uniform must be worn during training sessions.
  3. Use of training equipment must follow safety protocols.
  4. Any form of harassment or misconduct will result in disciplinary action.
Data Privacy
  1. Personal information will be used solely for training and employment purposes.
  2. Data will be protected in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.
  3. Photos and videos may be taken for training and promotional purposes.
  4. Students have the right to access and correct their personal information.
Fees and Payments
  1. Training fees must be paid according to the scheduled payment plan.
  2. Refunds are subject to TESDA's refund policy.
  3. Additional fees may apply for replacement of IDs or certificates.
  4. Scholarship grants have specific maintenance requirements.
Certification
  1. Certification is awarded upon successful completion of all requirements.
  2. National Certificates (NC) are valid for 5 years.
  3. Certificates can be verified through the TESDA online system.
  4. Replacement certificates require proper documentation and fees.
