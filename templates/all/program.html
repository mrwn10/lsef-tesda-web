Bookkeeping Program Details

Course Description

This program covers the competencies required to maintain books of accounts, prepare financial reports, and review internal control systems in various business environments.

Learning Outcomes

  • Post transactions to the general ledger
  • Prepare trial balance and basic financial statements
  • Review internal control systems
  • Process payroll and tax documents
  • Use accounting software applications

Requirements

  • High school diploma or ALS equivalent
  • Birth certificate (NSO/PSA authenticated)
  • 2x2 ID pictures (white background)
  • Barangay clearance
  • Medical certificate

Career Opportunities

  • Bookkeeper
  • Accounting Assistant
  • Accounts Payable/Receivable Clerk
  • Payroll Clerk
  • Auditing Clerk

Additional Information

Duration: 300 hours (10 weeks full-time)

Assessment: Written test and practical demonstration

Certification: National Certificate (NC) upon completion

Food and Beverages Program Details

Course Description

This program provides the knowledge and skills needed to deliver professional food and beverage service in restaurants, hotels, resorts, and other hospitality establishments.

Learning Outcomes

  • Prepare dining areas for service
  • Welcome guests and take food/beverage orders
  • Serve food and beverages professionally
  • Provide room service
  • Handle guest complaints
  • Process payments

Requirements

  • High school diploma or ALS equivalent
  • Birth certificate (NSO/PSA authenticated)
  • 2x2 ID pictures (white background)
  • Barangay clearance
  • Medical certificate (fit to work)

Career Opportunities

  • Restaurant Crew
  • Barista
  • Room Service Attendant
  • Banquet Server

Additional Information

Assessment: Written test and practical demonstration

Certification: National Certificate (NC) upon completion

Bread and Pastry Production Program Details

Course Description

This program provides the competencies needed to prepare and produce bakery and pastry products, cakes, and desserts in commercial food establishments.

Learning Outcomes

  • Prepare and produce bakery products
  • Prepare and produce pastry products
  • Prepare and present gateaux, tortes, and cakes
  • Prepare and display petits fours
  • Prepare and present desserts

Requirements

  • High school diploma or ALS equivalent
  • Birth certificate (NSO/PSA authenticated)
  • 2x2 ID pictures (white background)
  • Barangay clearance
  • Medical certificate (fit to handle food)

Career Opportunities

  • Baker
  • Pastry Cook
  • Cake Decorator
  • Dessert Chef
  • Bakery Shop Owner

Additional Information

Duration: 240 hours (8 weeks full-time)

Assessment: Written test and practical demonstration

Certification: National Certificate (NC) upon completion
